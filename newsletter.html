Industry digitalisation: study updates

 

 

Dear colleague, 

We are happy to share the most recent updates of the Smart Industrial Remoting study, implemented by PPMI for the European Commission. In the past few months, we have published the Experiment report, overviewing 5 digitalisation pilots in SMEs, and hosted a workshop, diving deeper into pilots' results. Besides, an insights post shedding light on digitalisation best practices is now available online. 

Scroll down and check out the updates in more detail!

 

Recent events and publications

 

Digitalisation pilots report

 

A newly published report concludes on the results of 5 digitalisation pilots in industrial SMEs and outlines 27 horizontal takeaways learned from them. 

 

 

 

 

Pilots workshop takeaways

 

On 26 April, PPMI hosted a workshop to present the findings of digitalisation pilots. Key takeaways sum up the discussions of a recipe for successful digitalisation, trust-building and cooperation between SMEs and hubs.

 

 

Best practices insights

 

PPMI explored over 40 case studies across European companies to identify digitalisation best practices, agnostic of company size or the adopted technology. Our new insights post presents 8 best-practice principles for digitalisation.

 

 

Our next step is a Digitalisation toolbox, which will provide practical advice for companies and Digital Innovation Hubs. Keep an eye on our further updates!
